01 — Limestone

Earth Grounded in Stone

Sourced from the quarries of the Levant, the ivory limestone provides a thermal mass that breathes with the seasons. Its tactile surface absorbs the harshness of the sun, reflecting a soft, diffused glow into the inner courtyards.

02 — Steel & Glass

Transparency as Structure

Brushed stainless steel frames the immense glass apertures, dissolving the boundary between the internal sanctum and the external horizon. This is architecture that does not hide, but invites the world in.
